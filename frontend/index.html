Graduate Directory

FAST University opened its doors to its first batch of students in 2000 and has since been a pioneer in the field of higher education in Pakistan for technology and management sciences. FAST University is dedicated to building a bright future for its students by providing a quality education that fosters innovation and leadership.

This graduate directory provides comprehensive details on the achievements and academic excellence of its graduates. These students have shown their commitment to their field of study and to the community through their research, innovation, and service.
